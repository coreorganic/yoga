benefits


  • HATHA YOGA: Hatha Yoga makes the spine more flexible

  • KARMA YOGA: It is the path of selfless service. It is the source of true peace and happiness
  • BHAKTI YOGA: Bhakti Yoga is the system in which love and devotion to God are emphasized .
